Hummingbird Technologies


Inspired by Kiva Systems, a former mobile robotics startup now operating as Amazon Robotics, Hummingbird Technologies uses an innovative design to create their own automated multifunctional logistics robot with the potential for further optimizing operations at inventory distribution (four corner-mounted, independently steered and powered wheel modules) allow for fully decoupled translation and continuous rotation of the drive base.

As such, it is more maneuverable and cost-effective than its predecessor, ready to revolutionize the warehouse logistics robot.



Mechanical Design


To achieve fully-independent translation and rotation along with continuous rotation, existing swerve drivetrains often have two major drawbacks. Their innate mechanical complexity often leads to elevated part counts, and emphasis on packing factor and minimizing weight rarely allow design for manufacturability (DFM) or design for assembly (DFA) considerations to prevail. Because of the perceived industrial applicationâ€™s emphasis on durability and maintenance, the design sought to achieve the desired 500 lb load rating while minimizing part count and achieving better DFM results.

Replacing spur geartrains with timing belt reductions on the drive stage and an off-the-shelf planetary gearbox (linked to the wheel module by a 1:1 belt) on the turning stage reduced the amount of hardware and number of custom parts.

The wheel module itself also minimizes part count and eases manufacturing complexity by utilizing a single-piece aluminum extrusion sleeve with externally captured bearings.

Graphical User Interface


The robot is controlled with a gamepad or a joystick. It therefore needs no digital GUI for obtaining inputs.

However, we created an interface for outputing the speed and pointing direction of each module of the robot. This interface is meant for analyzing the performance of the robot. An operator can look at these outputs and potentially change the control parameters of the robot to achieve better performance.
