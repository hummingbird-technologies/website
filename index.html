Hummingbird Technologies


Inspired by Kiva Systems, a former mobile robotics startup now operating as Amazon Robotics, Hummingbird Technologies uses an innovative design to create their own automated multifunctional logistics robot with the potential for further optimizing operations at inventory distribution (four corner-mounted, independently steered and powered wheel modules) allow for fully decoupled translation and continuous rotation of the drive base.

As such, it is more maneuverable and cost-effective than its predecessor, ready to revolutionize the warehouse logistics robot.
